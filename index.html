02 / Roadmap Timeline

Built In
Phases

Program Structure

Our roadmap is designed as a phased journey so members grow from first-principles learning into applied rocketry, leadership, and mission-ready execution.

Phase 1 Duration: 4 Months

Sanrachna

Foundation Phase

Overview

Sanrachna focuses on building a strong foundation in rocketry through structured learning and collaboration. This phase is designed to equip members with essential technical knowledge, teamwork abilities, and personal growth alignment.

Key Focus Areas

  • Mastering fundamental rocketry concepts and core technical skills
  • Strengthening team building and collaborative problem-solving
  • Introduction to advanced rocketry principles and applications
  • Personal growth alignment with individual goals and club vision
